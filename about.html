Mission

The mission is to develop a fully digital platform to facilitate our customers to get Legal Services online. However, it is noted that there are legal boundaries to make the platform fully digital for Legal Services at present time. Providing hassle-free digital services as maximum as possible to our customers are our mission.

Vision

It is noticed that the customers are getting harrassed everytime to fulfill their Legal needs by visiting physically to the courts. Our vision is to reduced such harrassment by bringing a digital platform to serve them digitally as maximum as possible.
